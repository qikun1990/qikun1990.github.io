    A Campaign That Made Me Over $80,000 In Profit!



    Below is a campaign that made me over $80,000 in profit in 3 months. I'm going to show you exactly how to duplicate it! Please also keep in mind you'll make more money using strategies from this campaign and applying them to your own.This is more of a case study to show you what i did.
    ----

    Offer: MyWebFace
    Traffic Source: Facebook


    MyWebFace:
    The offer is a toolbar download, which allows users to take photos of themselves and edit them into cartoons.

    The Main Secret of This Campaign
    Instead of marketing the image editing software toolbar as cartoon editing software, i targeted niches and advertised it within a niche. My biggest niche was Vampires. From research i know girls 13-22 are highly involved in the Vampire/Twilight craze, the reach is enormous. Almost every girl that age knows of Twilight, and a good % of them are fans, or atleast are secret fans of the Twilight series.

    Here's What I Did With Vampires
    I advertised to: Females, in the United States, 13 - 20 years old. (I mixed up many different ads with ages, some 13-18, 19 - 22, etcâ€¦)




    Here's My Facebook Ad


    Images Are Your Best Friend
    For this campaign you need to use IMAGES, and split test with many different images. Your images which give you clicks as high as 1 dollar, or as low as 1 cent. You need to find what images will give you the cheap clicks! I used over 1000 different images, i got them from everywhere!

    Some image sources:
    -images.google.com
    -gettyimages.com
    -deviantart.com (a secret gem of mine!)

    Volume
    Once you get it working, you need to run volume! Try different countries, offers, niches and any ideas you have to expand; try it out!


    Many Cartoon Toolbar Offers
    There are MANY companies for this image editing toolbar offers! You don't just have to use MyWebFace, i highly suggest split testing. Certain toolbars installed better for certain niches, so you need to test to see which is performing best!

    Countries
    All the different offers, have toolbars for many different countries: United States, Canada, United Kingdon, Australia, New Zealand, Mexico and many many more!
    You need to promote in ALL countries to make as much money as possible. Also different countries will yield different ROI's!

    Change Up Your Niches
    For this offer the niche's are ENDLESS! You can literally make millions by changing up the niches.
    YOU DON'T HAVE TO PROMOTE VAMPIRES TO GIRLS!
    Mix it up, be creative and you'll make more money! Promote:
    - Transformers to guys
    - Monkeys to animal lovers
    etc...
    These are just some half ass ideas i just came up with, but if you take some time you can really think of some good ideas!

    Getting Past Facebook
    Facebook is sketchy with approving toolbars!.They say they don't allow it, but i get approved quite often. WAY more often then compared to rebills, mobile (ringtones), and even basic lead gen forms.BUT, sometimes you won't get approved so you have to cloak.
    To cloak just make your campaign, then switch the links to something basic that will get approved (it obviously has to relate to your ad, so do some google searching for something relative). Once you've switch the links, submit your ads, then pause them. Now you won't be wasting any cash on the clicks.

    Profit! (Is this all worth your time?)

    Cloaking VS Changing Destination URL:

    I personally am a big fan of changing the destination URL. I've only had one account banned and that was during the Facebook prime banning days about 8 months ago. I switch destination URLs all he time and don't have nearly as many issues as you'd think.

    Cloaking is when your ad is redirected to another URL if it comes from a "Facebook reviewers IP". There are also geo cloaking scripts for certain states and countries where facebook reviewers reside.

    ----

    I often split test compliant and non compliant offers such as IQ quizzes. Atleast 50% of the time the compliant offer converts better. If you want to play it safe, only use compliant offers ofcourse, but this could be the difference between a campaign that profits vs a campaign that doesn't.
