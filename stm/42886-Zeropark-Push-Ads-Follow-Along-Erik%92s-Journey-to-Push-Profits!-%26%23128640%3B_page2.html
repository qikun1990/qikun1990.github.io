    Hi Erik! Thanks for the great follow-along.
    Been working with finance offers in Latin America too. Made some results from certain ad networks such as PropellerAds, Adsterra, Megapush etc. Never tried ZeroPark, but looking forward to try them out

    It seems like you are using creatives with Spanish language, not Latin (I can be wrong). If so, try to test same creatives with two other language variations. For me, I found out that some creatives may work with original Spanish, and some may work with Mexican Spanish. Kinda weird
    Overall, I made ~20 creatives for MX campaign. Some benefits that i found:
    1) Long-live creatives. If you make the creatives that is really rocking for these offers, they can live for months. Like mine
    2) Payout bumps due to volumes. I got my first payout increase after only one week working with this advertiser! And never got quality abuse or smth.
    3) Stable-working offers. Only one offer was going good the whole month. Not many verticals can boast such performance.
    4) ROI was...acceptable. Just a little flash from tracker:

    So if you guys going to scale with this vertical I recommend to upload many creatives (15+) and find sources with lower bids (it's difficult rn because push traffic is pumping up, but possible)
