    Rookie Hits +5500 conversions: Pin Submit - Tier 2 - One FB Account - No Cloaking

    Not very often I try to help and support starting mediabuyers to make decent money via sweeps on Facebook. Since budgets are most of time not that big for a starter I am taking them back to the basics. No cost on spytools, tracking software or other "unnecessary" costs, just mediabuy spend! A method I am fancying for about 5 years now and still is relevant to the affiliate marketing business.

    This person had some experience with FB ad manager and running campaigns for companies. She quit her job, I took her by hand and we started around Q4 - 2017.

    Tools;
    - 1 account
    - fb ad manager
    - cake login of advertiser

    Setup;
    - fresh and clean domain - > hosting the survey prelander (4 simple questions regarding groceries/shopping)
    - after the 4 questions, CTA button containing affiliate link

    This way you can put the fresh domain link in FB ad manager, which never will be blocked or raise flags from start (if prelander is clean, not agressive and doesn't contain pop-up boxes, push notifications or other disturbing stuff)

    Campaign details;
    - pin submit
    - costa rica
    - two supermarket voucher campaigns
    - targeting women 35+, broad (no interest)
    - angle something like - "Tap Here, We Might Pay Your Groceries This Month"

    Results; (see image below)
    - more than 5500 conversions
    - ROI 100% +
    - xx/xxx daily income
    - account and campaign still running (!!!) - account is 216 days old
    - average cpc 0,02 cents



    Learnings;
    - cloaking is not a must (yes, I keep repeating this over and over again)
    - try to run less agressive and your campaign and account will last long(er)
    - spread your budgets on multiple accounts - not 500 euro/day on 1 account, better 100 euro/day on 5 accounts each
    - having a solid account is key - if your foundation is stable you can easily build a house brick by brick
    - be patient - try to learn the basics of affiliate marketing/pin submits/facebook etc before you think of money & scaling

    At the moment she runs several campaigns on multiple accounts (invest -> learn -> optimize -> make profit -> invest a bit more -> expand -> more profit et etc).
