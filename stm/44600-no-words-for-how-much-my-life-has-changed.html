    no words for how much my life has changed

    Hi everyone,

    I wanted to post my story here because I wanted to let everyone know how much my life has changed in the past 2 months. I joined this board and started affiliate marketing April 1st. Here are my results for the past 10 days across 2 voluums.



    Together, our revenue has been around $120K USD and profits around $77k in 10 days.


    Long story short, I started off doing sweeps in Kenya. I met someone on here who I asked if they would teach me and in return I would give him a permanent share of my profits. After teaching me for awhile, we found out we got along really well in terms of complimentary skill sets and then we decided to move forward and become partners. Currently, we have grown our team to around 20 people.

    4 small stories you guys might enjoy:
    1) There was one night after multiple nights of 20 hour per day grinds, we were losing about $1K per day and trying to juggle a bunch of problems. I remember lying in bed and just crying because nothing was working. I called him and I heard on the other side of the call how his voice was sore from also crying. We talked about how we need to realign our priorities and not destroy ourselves in the process. There is always money to be made and problems to solve. I will always remember this day for the rest of my life.

    2) Along this process, I was getting job interviews. I got an offer for my dream job at an annual salary $120k usd for a big oil company. But my heart wasn't in it anymore. Even though we weren't making money at the time I believed in us. I turned down that job offer and never regretted it and I know I never will.

    3) One time, one of our wires came in super late. We were playing float super super hard meaning that essentially every day the bank account dropped to less than 1K while our budgets for campaigns were >10K. I ended up wiring around 10k over. Which ties in with...

    4)I have actually never met this guy. I barely have an idea of what he looks like. I'm actually about to meet him this weekend. Wish me luck everyone.

    Finally, I would like to personally thank STM. Without you guys, I would've never met this amazing person in my life.
    My life has turned upside down in the best way in the past 2 months.
