    [NEW TRAINING] Introducing the Facebook Master Class

    Hi STM

    Eric Dyck here. As I hope you've noticed, we've been crazy busy at iStack Training. Our live event FBML was a smash hit and pretty seriously well reviewed by the attendees and viewers of the video. For that event we partnered with Patrick Dermak from Adbaker in Germany.

    Since FBML we've been hard at work on our next project and now we're ready to open up the Facebook Master Class for its first hundred students. The FBMC is the ultimate training for Facebook Ad Buyers.

    If you have been waiting for this course and know what we're about, then don't wait:

    CLAIM YOUR SEAT IN THE FACEBOOK MASTER CLASS

    If you want more information, please go check it out and read up on some of Patrick's credentials. I have to say, finding someone to follow the amazing work @Zeno does with the 6 Week AMC, should have been challenging. Luckily @phil introduced us in my first week on the job and the bromance was born Not only is Patrick the real deal when it comes to running high scale, high profit campaigns on Facebook, he's also a seriously polished and well thought out teacher. Every person who's seen the full curriculum is pretty impressed with the level of content here.

    Speaking of the level, this course is a little different than the 6 Week AMC. It's less for newbs who are looking to get into affiliate marketing (even though the AMC attracts its fair share of vets looking for a new edge), and more for people who are already spending on Facebook, or at at least familiar enough with media buying on the platform that they can apply the knowledge to campaigns they are already running, or know how to run. That said, the strategies in the FBMC can help you scale and profit whether you're spending $1000 a month or $1000 an hour.

    Basically Patrick's taken the last 4 years of building a leading agency and boiled down all the most valuable learnings into 5 weeks of content. So what's the 6th week you might ask? Glad you did.

    Just like the last 6 Week AMC - Foundation + Acceleration, we've decided to add in 5 bonus modules if you buy this course in its first run. We really worked to include Acceleration Modules that stand alone as super valuable skills to aid you in your quest of being a master of white hat Facebook.


    • James Van Elswyk - Purple Leads - Stop Guessing: Testing Methods that Save me Millions
    • Mo Ali Aguel - 8 Figure eCommerce - Rapid Prototype: Ecommerce
    • Hugh Hancock - STM Forum - Facebook Automation Roadmap
    • Drew Eric Whitman - Dr. Direct Diagnoses the Best Facebook Ads
    • Paul Jeyapal - Next Level Ad Spying: The Invisible Advantage


    So go have a look, and let me know if you have any questions. This course is our very first run on a new platform, so we're only accepting 100 students and registration is only open until August 30th, so if you want to get on board for this beauty, at our introductory rate (about half of 6 Week AMC), then don't delay.


    CLAIM YOUR SEAT IN THE FACEBOOK MASTER CLASS

    Thanks!

    PS. if you're a black hatter effected by some of Facebook's recent aggressive moves against you, then definitely take a look. The strategies and tactics in this course can be far more profitable in the long run!
